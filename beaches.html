Best Time to Visit Philippine Beaches

Dry Season

November to May

  • Best for beach activities
  • Sunny and warm weather
  • Perfect for island hopping
  • Calm seas
Surfing Season

June to October

  • Best for surfing (Siargao)
  • Fewer tourists
  • Better deals on resorts
  • Lush green landscapes
